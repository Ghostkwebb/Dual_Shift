## 🎮 What Is Dual Shift?

**Dual Shift** isn't about flashy cutscenes or pay-to-win shortcuts. It's a love letter to the arcade era — a game about that pure **flow state** where the world moves fast, the music pumps, and your reflexes take over completely.

It starts simple. You run, you jump. Then the speed kicks in. The enemies swarm. Survival demands you master the **Dual Shift** — snap between the Upper and Lower lanes in a split second to dodge traps, and unleash a **Dash Strike** to shatter enemies blocking your path.

> *How fast can you actually think?*

## ✨ Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| ⚔️ **Combat Runner** | Don't just dodge — **strike back.** Timing your Dash Strike is the only way to clear the road ahead. |
| 🧠 **"Zone" Gameplay** | Two lanes. Infinite danger. Engineered to push your focus to the absolute limit. |
| 📈 **Dynamic Difficulty** | The further you run, the wilder it gets. The game evolves with your skill level — there is no ceiling. |
| 🎨 **Hand-Crafted Pixel Art** | A vibrant, retro aesthetic that stays crisp and readable even at breakneck speeds. |
| 🏆 **Pure Skill** | No gems. No continues. No shortcuts. If you set a high score, **you earned it.** |

## 🕹️ Controls

### Mobile
| Action | Input |
|---|---|
| **Shift Lane** | **tap Left of Screen** |
| **Dash Strike** | **Tap Right of Screen** |

### PC (itch.io)
| Action | Key(s) |
|---|---|
| **Shift Lane** | `Space` |
| **Dash Strike** | `Left Click` |
